How open

Open

  • Research outputs — hypotheses, analysis summaries, evidence
  • Computational pipelines (Apache 2.0)
  • Data sources — public repositories and accessions
  • Preprints on bioRxiv (CC-BY 4.0)
  • Selection criteria and cohort decisions
  • GitHub discussion and review history

Not open

  • Marvin's core architecture and training methodology
  • Internal review deliberations (outputs are public; process is not)
  • Submission details for unselected projects

Iluvatar takes no IP on findings produced through the initiative. Outputs belong to the scientific commons. The line is at outputs vs. engine: everything Marvin produces is open; how Marvin works internally is commercial.
