About.

Iluvatar Labs is building closed-loop autonomous systems you can actually trust.

Trust is the bottleneck for autonomous AI.

The technology exists. The systems work. The reason autonomous AI isn't already everywhere is that LLMs are opaque by design. You can't always watch one work and know what it will do, or look at what it did and know why. That opacity is the trust gap.

Closing it takes rigorous control over how a model behaves while it runs. Live monitoring, in-flight adjustments, baseline capabilities preserved across every training transformation. That is the Actuator path.

Or meaningful auditability of what a model produces. Every result, interpretation, and decision versioned and reviewable. The work a real scientist would show. That is the Marvin path.
