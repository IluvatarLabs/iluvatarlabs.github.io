IORI theme — pick a direction.

Five candidate palettes, each in the spirit of meh_alt2: three thematically related surfaces, two darker shades plus one warm-light section, no abrupt light/dark alternation. Each row shows the same three surface roles (hero, mid-band, light section) plus an accent chip. Compare the mood and the family relationship. None of these reuse colors already on the site.

A · Oxblood & bone
Manuscript · library · ledger

Deep saturated oxblood as the hero, near-black wine for the mid-band, warm bone for the third section. Reads as a 19th-century scientific journal — old paper, leather binding, ink. Amber gold accent feels like aged brass on a microscope. Distinct from coral (warmer, redder, much darker) and from About's indigo (no blue at all).
